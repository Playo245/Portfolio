What is our app

Our app will show cycle tracks that are friendly towards cyclists. We will include the price of bus tickets, tolls in motorways and the Dublin City bikes. We will also include a status on each route you choose, to show how much traffic there is. There'll be tourist suggestions on our map. We will aim to provide an estimated cost for each mode of transport to give the user options of which is cost-effective.


Comparison

The main difference between us and Google Maps is that we will give updates to warn drivers, cyclists, motor cyclists and pedestrians about crashes or roadworks so that they can change routes. Because we look after Ireland, we will be more accurate than Google Maps with our GPS. We also aim to update the traffic status constantly. This will ensure that users can be up to date with the traffic so they can change their routes.
