FAQ

Q. Where could I get the app?

A. Depends on what phone you are using, For iPhone, the AppStore and for Android, Google Play Store.


Q. Should I use this over Google Maps?

A. If not in Ireland, Yes, But if you are in Ireland we recommend using our app as it is directly focused on Ireland.


Q. Is your app cyclist friendly?

A. Yes, it is. It will show cycle tracks around Ireland and will also show prices for Dublin City bikes or any rentals nearby.


Q. Will the app show the information about my destination?

A. Yes, there will be a short description of your destination and what facilities might be there.
